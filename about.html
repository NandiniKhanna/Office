Our Strength

Our strength lies in finding the appropriate candidate for our client (be it through headhunting, our database or advertising)

We believe in partnering with our client and not just posting resumes.

Therefore we do part of our client’s job by doing a preliminary round of interview and also reference checks, thus diminishing the client’s workload and time spent on hiring.

Our strength also lies in the data that we have collected over a period of time based on our selection process. Simultaneously we have followed the career growth of our potential candidates, this has helped us to continuously edit and upgrade our data with the right profiles.


Our Working – An Overview

  • Identification of client’s need through assessing the organization and job profile.

  • Resume Screening and selection on basis of preliminary information provided by our client.

  • Screening candidates through preliminary test & interviews.

  • National/International executive hunt if a required profile is not in our databank.
